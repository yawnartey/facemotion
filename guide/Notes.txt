FACS = Facial Action Coding System
AU = Action Units 

Guide/Ref docs for FACS -> https://imotions.com/blog/learning/research-fundamentals/facial-action-coding-system/?srsltid=AfmBOop6FrcYg617XJXyW790awSTn1HkQr1QQQVlWNdfnQsN-dYG99g2

AU1: Inner brow raiser. Frontalis (medial). Frontalis is the facial muscles located in the forehead
AU2: Outer brow raiser. Frontalis (lateral). Facial muscle located in the forehead
AU4: Brow lowerer. Corrugator supercilii. Located at the inner portion of the eyebrow
AU5: Upper lid raiser. Levator palpebrae superioris (eyes wide). Muscle located at the upper eyelid
AU6: Cheek raiser. Orbicularis oculi(raises cheeks). Muscle at the area beneath the eyelid skin
AU7: Lid tightner. Orbicularis oculi (squint)
AU9: Nose wrinkler / upper lips raiser. nasalis / levator labii superioris alaeque nasi.
AU12: Lip corner puller(smile). Zygomaticus major. Muscle that extends diagonally from the cheekbones to the corners of the mouth
AU15: Lip corner depressor. Depressor anguli oris. Muscle that pulls the corner of the mouth downward and outward
AU18: Lip puckerer (pout). Orbicularis oris. Muscle located at either side of the lower jaw
AU20: Lip stretcher (horizontal pull). Risorius (stretches mouth)
AU23: Lip tightner. Orbicularis oris(narrow lips)
AU24: Lip pressor. Orbicularis oris (presses lips together)
AU26: Jaw drop. Mandible depression / jaw‑opening muscles (mouth open) 


Angry = AU4 + AU7 + AU23 + AU24
    - Knit the brows together & press/clench the lips
    - Pull the inner brows towards the nose and press the lips firmly together
Surprised = AU1 + AU2 + AU5 + AU26
    - Open the eyes wide and drop the jaw
    - Keep the eyes wide open and let the jaw open slightly
Disgust = AU9 + AU15
    - Raise the upper lips and draw the eyebrows together
    - Upper lips should be curled a bit towards the nose
Fear = AU1 + AU2 + AU4 + AU5 + AU7 + AU20
    - Squeeze eyes & clench mouth
    - Combine high brow raise with tense, wide/strained eyes
    - Also stretch the mouth a little bit
Smile = AU12 + AU6
    - Pull the corners of the lips up 
    - Mouth drawn wide
    - Lift corners of the mouth and let the cheeks rise 
Sadness = AU1 + AU4 + AU15
    - Corners of the mouth drawn downwards
    - Slight inner-brow raise
Pout = AU18 + AU24 
    - Mouth pursed
    - Round the lips forward and press gently
Puff cheeks = 
    - Cheeks inflated
    - Close the lips and fill it with air 
    - Avoid movement of the jaw
Smile + Cheeks = AU12 + cheeks + (maybe AU6)
    - Combine lip corner pull with  lifting of the cheek





Internal Testing Plans 
- Each user (or tester) to test a phase 
- Each phase test last approximately 30mins


Flow
- Explain what the testing is going to be about 
- Explain their positioning of the camera
	- camera needs to be adjusted properly
	- they should look at their own avatar for optimum score point
- Mention that the exercise targets their muscle groups, so the focus should be:
	- moving the muscles on their face and not just smiling or carrying out the exercise
	- moving the muscles help improves their score
- Demonstrate the exercise
